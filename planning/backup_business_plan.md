# Algorium UK - Business & Development Plan

## 1. Executive Summary
Algorium UK is a premium digital agency based in the UK, specializing in high-end design, AI-integrated marketing, and custom software solutions. Our goal is to provide cutting-edge digital transformation for businesses looking to lead in the modern era.

## 2. Core Services
- **Graphic Design:** UI/UX, Branding, Visual Identity.
- **Mobile Applications:** High-performance iOS and Android development.
- **Web Development:** Modern, SEO-optimized, and fast web ecosystems.
- **AI-Centric Marketing:** Data-driven strategies, AI-powered content, and marketing automation.
- **Design & Software Support:** Ongoing maintenance, technical consultancy, and performance optimization.

## 3. Visual Identity & Design Principles (Kota-inspired)
- **Aesthetics:** Ultra-Minimal, High-Contrast, Bold Typography.
- **Colors:** Deep Charcoal (#1a1a1a) for depth, Pure White (#ffffff) for space, and **Brand Orange (#FE532D)** as the prestige accent.
- **Typography:** Bold Sans-serif (Outfit/Inter) mixed with Serif Italic for emotional highlights. Extreme size differences between headers and body.
- **Micro-interactions:** Magnetic buttons, spotlight reveals, and floating project images on hover.
- **Core Values:** "Negative Space is a feature", clarity, and premium feel.

## 4. Technical Strategy (Next-Gen Architecture)
- **Frontend:** Next.js (App Router) on Vercel/Edge for ultimate speed.
- **Backend:** **Hono.js** (Node/Edge Runtime) - Ultra-fast, lightweight API.
- **Styling:** Vanilla CSS foundations for Kota-style minimal performance.
- **Data Flow:** Type-safe JSON API with Zod validation.
- **Architecture:** "Composable & Edge-First" - built to scale globally with minimal latency.
