# Algorium UK - Business & Development Plan

## 1. Executive Summary
Algorium UK is a premium digital agency based in the UK, specializing in high-end design, AI-integrated marketing, and custom software solutions. Our goal is to provide cutting-edge digital transformation for businesses looking to lead in the modern era.

## 2. Core Services
- **Graphic Design:** UI/UX, Branding, Visual Identity.
- **Mobile Applications:** High-performance iOS and Android development.
- **Web Development:** Modern, SEO-optimized, and fast web ecosystems.
- **AI-Centric Marketing:** Data-driven strategies, AI-powered content, and marketing automation.
- **Design & Software Support:** Ongoing maintenance, technical consultancy, and performance optimization.

## 3. Visual Identity & Design Principles
- **Aesthetics:** Premium, Professional, High-Tech.
- **Colors:** Deep Blues, Charcoal, Electric Blue/Purple accents.
- **Typography:** Inter, Outfit (Modern Sans Serif).
- **Core Values:** Innovation, Reliability, Aesthetic Excellence.

## 4. Technical Strategy
- **Frontend:** Next.js (App Router) for high performance and SEO.
- **Styling:** Vanilla CSS for maximum flexibility and rich UI.
- **Backend:** Node.js / Next.js API Routes (if unified) or separate service (if complex).
- **Design:** Pencil tools for rapid prototyping and validation.

## 5. Development Phases
1. **Phase 1: Planning & Design (Pencil)**
2. **Phase 2: Foundation (Styles & Layout)**
3. **Phase 3: Component Development**
4. **Phase 4: API & Integration**
5. **Phase 5: Refinement & Launch**
